About Tool Picks

We help you buy the right tool the first time.

Tool Picks is an independent review site. We research, compare, and recommend power tools. We're not sponsored by any brand. We don't accept free products in exchange for favorable reviews. Every recommendation is based on spec analysis, cross-referenced user reviews, and expert input.

Our recommendations are supported by reader purchases through affiliate links. When you buy a tool through one of our links, we earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you. This is how we keep the site free and ad-free.
